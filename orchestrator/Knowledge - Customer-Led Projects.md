# Customer-Led Projects

## Overview

Customer-Led Projects are initiatives initiated by customer departments and agencies in support of their program objectives. SSC provides support, usually in the form of infrastructure services.

---

## Key Characteristics

| Aspect | Customer-Led Project |
|--------|---------------------|
| **Stages** | 5 (no Stage 1: Investment) |
| **Authority** | Customer department |
| **Cost Recovery** | Full recovery from customer |
| **Funding** | Recovery Agreements or TB Submissions |
| **Gate 1** | Not applicable |

---

## Project Lifecycle

### Stages for Customer-Led Projects

1. **Stage 0: Intake and Concept** → Gate 0
2. **Stage 2: Preliminary Planning** → Gate 2
3. **Stage 3: Detailed Planning** → Gate 3
4. **Stage 4: Execution** → Gate 4
5. **Stage 5: Deployment and Closeout** → Gate 5

---

## Key Differences from SSC-Led Projects

| Aspect | SSC-Led | Customer-Led |
|--------|---------|--------------|
| **Stages** | 6 | 5 |
| **Stage 1 (Investment)** | Required | Not applicable |
| **Gate 1 (FIMB)** | Required | Not applicable |
| **Gate 0** | Required | Required |
| **PCRA** | SSC prepares | Customer provides |
| **Business Case** | Required | Customer provides |
| **Funding** | Internal or TB | Recovery from customer |
| **Project Authority** | SSC | Customer |

---

## PCRA Requirements

### Customer Responsibility
- **Customer provides their PCRA** to confirm their project authority
- SSC does NOT prepare PCRA for Customer-Led projects
- Customer's PCRA applies to **entire project** including SSC component
- SSC verifies customer's authority before proceeding

### What SSC Validates
- Customer has appropriate project authority
- PCRA level matches customer's capacity
- Customer's governance is adequate

---

## Financial Requirements

### Cost Recovery
- Costs incurred by SSC are **fully recovered** from customer
- Signed **Recovery Agreements** required

### ePET Requirements
- Use **Enterprise Price Estimation Tool (ePET)**
- Use **Custom Pricing Module** (NOT Standard Pricing)
- Generate **Project Recovery Agreement (PRA)**

### Project Recovery Agreement (PRA)
- Created using ePET custom pricing
- Supporting evidence required for all cost elements
- Customer responsible for ensuring funding

---

## Project Change Requests (PCR)

### Special Requirements
- **Client Executive must review ALL PCRs** before presentation to PMB
- Changes apply to SSC component only
- Customer authority governs overall project changes

### Process
1. PM prepares PCR
2. Client Executive reviews
3. If approved, present to PMB
4. Customer handles their own governance

---

## Benefits Management

### SSC-Led Projects
- Benefits Management Program (BMP) leads
- Benefits Register maintained by SSC
- Benefits Realization Plan created

### Customer-Led Projects
- Customer owns benefits
- SSC supports customer outcomes
- Project Team collaborates with BMP to identify SSC outcomes

---

## Procurement

### For Customer-Led Projects
- Procurement may be handled by customer or SSC
- SSC procurement rules apply to SSC work
- Coordinate with Enterprise IT Procurement (EITP)

---

## Governance

### Board Progression
- **Gate 0**: 5P Board
- **Gates 2-5**: PMB (no FIMB/Gate 1)

### Client Executive Role
- Primary point of contact for customer
- Reviews all project materials
- Approves PCRs before PMB
- Represents customer interests

---

## Summary

Customer-Led projects are managed by the customer department, with SSC providing infrastructure and technical support. SSC manages its portion under the PGOF, but the overall project authority rests with the customer. Key points:

- 5 stages (no Investment Stage)
- Customer provides PCRA
- Full cost recovery required
- Client Executive reviews all changes
- PMB handles gates 2-5 (no FIMB)
